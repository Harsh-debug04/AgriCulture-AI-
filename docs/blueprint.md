# **App Name**: KrishiMitra AI

## Core Features:

- Crop Recommendation Tool: Based on soil analysis and weather conditions, provide intelligent recommendations on optimal crops to plant. The tool takes soil type, historical weather data, and market demand into account.
- Pest & Disease Identification: Allows farmers to upload images of potentially diseased plants, and uses AI to identify any diseases and suggest targeted treatment options.
- Weather Advisory Dashboard: Presents an intuitive dashboard showing local, real-time weather data and predictive weather advisories tailored for agricultural needs. Includes alerts for frost, heat waves, or excess rainfall.
- Market Price Monitoring: Aggregates and displays current market prices for crops across different regions, helping farmers make informed decisions about when and where to sell their produce. Includes historical price trends.
- AI Chatbot Assistant: An AI powered assistant answering all agriculture related queries.
- Community Forum: A community forum where farmers can ask questions and exchange practical agriculture experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#558B2F), symbolizing growth and nature.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a neutral and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#FF7043) used to highlight calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif, for an elegant feel; Body font: 'PT Sans' a humanist sans-serif, for clear readability.
- Use simple, outlined icons related to farming, weather, and market data.
- Employ a grid-based layout for orderly arrangement of dashboard elements and other info sections.
